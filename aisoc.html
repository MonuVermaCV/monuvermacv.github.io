About AISoC

AISoC is the Society for excellence in Artificial Intelligence based in KIIT Bhubaneswar. It is a community of students (supported by Prof. Murari Mandal and Prof. Vikas Hassija, School of Computer Engineering KIIT) passionate to learn, educate, and develop projects in the emerging fields of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. AISoC aims to provide opportunities for students enthusiastic about learning, discussing, and applying AI in different fields of study such as climate change, surveillance, healthcare, privacy, signal analysis, computer vision, natural language processing, speech analysis, etc. Our aim is to cultivate, support, and prepare students to learn AI for academic as well as industrial applications. We aim to also enrich the start-up incubation ecosystem and other AI based projects by students.

Activities in AISoC

We organize multiple learning oriented and fun events related to AI and its applications. Through these activities we aim to create, nurture, and support students to become familiar with the emerging field of AI. Activities of the Society:

  • Organising AI Workshops to offer introductory courses in AI, Lab sessions for learning, and on various latest developments in AI.
  • Regularly hold intra-KIIT challenges (with Prize) on several topics in AI.
  • Conduct regional and national level Hackathon (with Prize) on several relevant real-world problems to be solved with the help of AI.
  • Conduct Expert Talks: Invite top AI minds across India to present their work. Opportunities to interact with leaders in industry and academia.
  • Summer/Winter School of AI.
  • Maintain AI Blogs and regularly post articles to create awareness among the students about the latest developments in AI across the world.
  • Conduct Special Sessions: How to do AI research, how to write an AI paper, How to solve a real world industry problem.

Our Vision

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has already been around since decades. But due to the recent progress in Deep Learning, it is poised to disrupt our world in . The AI-enabled intelligent machines are enabling high-level cognitive processes like thinking, perceiving, learning, problem-solving, decision making. Along with advances in data collection, aggregation, analytics, computer processing power, AI presents opportunities to complement and supplement human intelligence and enrich the way people live and work. AISoC aims to be a launching pad for students eager to take up a research or industry career in AI. We recognize the high aspirations of students to excel in academia, industry, and entrepreneurship. Thus, we focus on catering to the various career directions in AI. The vision of the society is to provide content, guidance and practical experience to students of KIIT in building a community of like-minded individuals.

What is in Store for AISoC Members?

If you are looking to begin your journey in the field of AI, eager to learn, want to hone your skills, and collaborate on AI projects, then this Society is the place for you to join or become a member. We have a variety of events planned throughout each semester. You will get opportunities to connect with like-minded people and discuss and learn and collaborate, to drive the overall growth of the AI community at KIIT. Some of the benefits of joining this Society:

  • Developing Special Skills for job placements. Top companies like Meta, Apple, Amazon, Google, Microsoft, and many Start-ups require expert AI people in their team. We want the members of AISoC to collaboratively learn, become expert, and teach others in the field of AI.
  • Research internship, MS, and PhD opportunities in high-ranking universities such as NUS, University of Glasgow, University of New South Wales, NTU Singapore, etc.
  • Opportunity to collaborate and work with top researchers from national (IIT Delhi, IIIT Delhi, IIT jodhpur, ISM Dhanbad, BITS Pilani, IIT Ropar, IIIT Hyderabad) and international universities (NUS, University of Glasgow, University of new south Wales, NTU)
  • Industry internship opportunities in AI based research start-ups.
  • Participation opportunity in AI hackathons across India.
  • Opportunity to meet and interact with AI industry leaders.
  • You will be part of a team that will make learning AI fun :-)

Hi There...Welcome to my personal webpage!

I am Monu Verma, a Computer Vision Scientist. I am working as a Postdoctoral Associate at the University of Miami, FL, USA with Prof. Mohamed Abdel-Mottaleb.

My current research is in Neural Architecture Search, Federated Learning, Transformers, Medical Image Processing, Affective Computing, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision. My research expertise is particularly notable in the areas of Human Emotion Analysis, encompassing facial expression recognition and micro-expression analysis. Additionally, I have made significant contributions to Hand Gesture Recognition and Medical Image/Video Analysis involving diverse multimodal data sources such as MRIs, X-rays, molecular information, demographical information, as well as electrical and magnetic signals.
